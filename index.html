Attractions

AXIS has descried a historical moment which will forever be remembered by the students of VNIT as well as all the participants of AXIS. The festival got the eminence to be inaugurated by honored Late Dr. A P J Abdul Kalam , Former President of India.
The campus was filled with enormous positive energy and his words of sagacity will forever be cherished by the spectators of AXIS. Forbye, an astounding performance of an emerging form of art called “Tron Dance” was witnessed. This collaboration of technology and entertainment is one of a kind. Another major episode has been Quora meet-up.
Writers from the most famous online learning and knowledge portal held an interactive session with students and participants of AXIS, which became an indelible evening filled with ecstasy. Another manoeuvre was attained when an invigorating series of performances, guest lectures and proshows left the audiences wheezing and cheering for more. The festival was inaugurated by Dr. Ashish Saraf, Vice President,Industry Development, Strategic Partnerships & Offsets, Airbus India as our Chief Guest and Mr. Sachin Kurve, the District Collector of Nagpur as the Guest of Honour. The prestigious night came to a conclusion by the take over of laser lights in Laser Show to create an indefatigable spirit. Guest lecture by Dr. Amish Tripathi, author of the famed Shiva Trilogy, was witnessed by a huge crowd who were all praises for the wit and wisdom they gained during interaction with him. Finally, the culminating act of international Visual DJ Afishal, who has taken DJ’ing to a different level, turned out to be an enthralling performance, leaving the audiences stunned.

Events

AXIS boasts of 30+ events! For those who have the flair to employ and prove their technical skills, AXIS bestows a perfect platform. From events intended for school students, to Robotics, construction, design, software, management and many more on the spot events, AXIS has an opportunity for every enthusiast to have an enriching experience while learning cutting edge technology, and earning amazing prizes too!

For all the young creative minds and innovative pioneers -Dexter and Junior Scientist provide the opportunity to explore the genius inside you. Enhance your endowments and get ready for a whole new interactive experience which will groom you with all the versatility to face a competitive world ahead.

For all the Automation and Robotics maniacs, AXIS hosts events like RoboWars, Mechatryst, Aquahunt, Autobot and Robo Saviour. If roaring sounds of engines, high speed jumping off the ramps, drifts, drags and intergalactic battles are your thing, these are your downright destination.

Think you have the potential to change the world? Come, Construct and Design your ideas with events like Devise, Crepido, Paradeigma, Turboflux and Aquaskylark. Bridge the gap between galaxies, test your mechanical proficiency against gravity, device ground-breaking solutions to the most unprecedented challenges and appertain your physics and aerodynamics skills. All this, with an anticipation of taking home memorable prizes.

If coding has been your passion and animation has fascinated you since childhood, AXIS fronts events like Animazione, Cryptocrux, Insomnia and Posterolic in the software and electronics bracket. All night coding summons and encryption-decryption battles are sure to transfix your mind. Brandish your 3D, 2D and stick figure animation videos jazzing with multifarious characters to sweep judges off their feet and win attractice cash prizes.

Do you wonder what it would be like to be a part of the universe’s elite 1%? Participate in Wallstreet, Who’s the Boss, 221 B Baker Street, Freak-o-Matrix and Gamesutra to experience the world of brokers, stocks, shares and economic trade floor. Be the CEO of your own company, assess your astute observation skills and deductive reasoning but try not to freak out!

If none of these satisfy your taste, venture into the veridical world by being a part of Coll-in-coll, Kartavya and Techno.docx. Win a chance to intern at one of the biggest companies just by emblazoning your cognitive and analytical brilliance. Get encountered to real problems faced by NGOs and provide out of the box solutions to win social internships abroad.

Initiatives

We at AXIS, believe in treading the roads less taken, and that has made all the difference, not only to us but to many who deserve much better. Each year social initiatives inspire dedicated volunteers from all ages and walks of life, who come together to 'be the change they want to see'. Bleeding for a cause through blood donation drives, spreading the word for social awareness through heart-warming street plays like 'Bacche Hai Hum', AXIS is truly a Fest with a Heart.
